A little background info

Welcome to the Life of JJ, a website designed to showcase my life from my passions to my skills in five pages. But to begin with, who am I? Well, my name is Janarthanan Jathushan and I was born in Newham, East London on the 11th of March 2001. I've always had a passion in the creative arts which drove me to follow my passions and do Digital Arts BA at the University of Kent. From a young age I've had a interest in creating amazing visuals and immersive experinces from my small Youtube channel at the age of 15 on Movie Maker to editing professional level visual collages on Premier Pro. In terms of skills I would say that i have decent enough knowledge to be capabale of softwares in graphic design and 3D modelling, however I'm always seeking to further improve my knowledge of the softwares and learn new skills that may help me in my desired field of Digital Arts.

Album: Isolation


In this album I collected a range of photos I have taken at various stages of my life that all have the same one theme in common: Isolation. I felt this theme was fitting for the era where technology and disease has distanced us all from the everyday routine of regular life where everyone is isolated from each other with quarantine and lockdown rules meaning everyone feels isolation in all their unique circumstances. But what does Isolation mean to me? Well I have a strong attachment to Isolation with my experience of having Coronavirus in making this project and my struggle with depression and anxiety.


In the Images below I explore the concept of Isolation in a few images to show what it feels like to me and my connection to it. I used images of nature to capture the initial peace and serenity found in Isolation which later grows into and emptiness and loneliness that I tried to show in my images of Architecture. The portraits I took all show a variety of emotions and poses to explore a person’s journey through isolation in whatever context and include no group pictures and such.

Frank Ocean - Nights Visual Collage

I initially began this project in January 2019 and published it in February 2019. In this small project I set about trying to create a visual montage using various videos on the internet to create a music video for one of my favourite songs ‘Nights’. After hearing this song, multiple times during revision I found myself filled with creative visions for potentials of a music video for this but due to having to juggle my A-Level mocks and Year 13 of sixth form I found myself unable to find time to film anything.

After searching on YouTube for edits/ music videos for the song but was unable to find one that captured my creative vision and so instead I decided to use videos I found from YouTube alongside my Premier Pro craft to make a visual collage to accompany the music. I also realised that this would allow me to enhance my portfolio and my Premier Pro skills so one day I could use it to showcase my talent as well as a means of stress relief from revision.
